2. contoh dialog expression stance, proposing,admitting


Kelas: SD
Kategori: expression
Kata kunci: expression stance, proposing, admitting
Pembahasan:

Example of expression stance:
- I think he's good.
- In my opinion, they'll win

Example of proposing:
- I have a proposition for you.
- will you join our team?

Example of admitting:
- Sorry, it's my fault.
- I have to admit that I made a mistake

Dialogue:
Marc: Dude, what's your score of your last Math test?
Ali: Not good, mate. I had to admit it was the worst score I've ever had.
Marc: Sorry about that. How come?
Ali: I think I didn't study the whole lessons. How about yours?
M: not good either, only 75. Worse than my history.
A: Ah, that's still a fine result. You're not bad at math. I heard the average score in school is only 60.
M: Is it? Hmm, I've got an idea and a proposition for you.
A; Sounds intriguing, what is it?
M: Will you possibly help me learn history? You're very good at social science.
 then, I'll do the same for math
A: Sure, that's fine.

37. Dialog gabungan, making complaint, proposing, request, possibility, giving instruction


Discussion

1. There are some common ways to describe problems and concerns.

- To show an excess of something you can use these patterns:

too + adjective. For example: The noodle is too soft.

too + adverb. For example: You speak too loudly.

- To show that something is lacking you can use this pattern:

not + adjective + enough. For example: The AC in this room is not cold enough.

Remember that when making a complaint, we have to be polite. You can use these phrases to begin your sentence:

- Sorry to bother you, but...

- I hate to say this, but...

- I don't usually complain, but...

- Excuse me. Do you have a moment?

Answer:

Let's see how we can combine making a complaint, proposing, request, possibility, and giving instruction in this dialogue. The dialogue is between a manager and her assistant in an advertising company. Rana is the supervisor and Nanda is the assistant.

Rana: Nanda, sorry to bother you. Do you have a moment?

Nanda: Yes, is there a problem?

Rana: I hate to say this, but one of the client just called me about the new design. They said they haven't received the file. (Rana is making a complaint)

Nanda: I apologize. I haven't finalized the detail with our graphic design department.

Rana: Would it be possible to finish it by tonight?

Nanda: I'm sorry, but I think it's a bit too difficult because one of our staffs is taking a day off today. I would be able to finish it by tomorrow afternoon, if it's okay. (Nanda is proposing a new deadline)

Rena: That's unfortunate, but I think there's nothing we can do. The client might complain though. (Rena is stating a possibility)

Nanda: I understand. Is there anything I can do?

Rena: Could you send an email to our other client who asked for a new design for their website? (Rena is making a request)

Nanda: Sure, no problem.

Rena: Please send some of our best design templates to them and ask whether they have any preference for their website or they would like us to decide everything. You can also cc me on the email. (Rena is giving a instruction)

Nanda: Got it! I'll send them the email right away.

Rena: Thanks, Nanda!

39. tolong buatkan contoh dialog dari ke lima ini ,complaint, proposing, request, possibility instruction


Pembahasan:

Sebelum membuat dialog, kita sebaiknya mengetahui ekspresi - ekspresi yang digunakan untuk mengindikasikan complaint, proposing, request, possibility, instruction. Nah, berikut ini kakak berikan beberapa contoh expression of complaining, proposing, requesting, possibility, dan giving instruction.

1. Expression of complaining

- I want to complain about ...

- There's something wrong with ...

- There seems to be a problem with ...

- There is a problem about ...

- There appears to be something wrong with ...

2. Expression of proposing

- i propose that ...

- let's ...

- why don't we ... ?

- How about ... ?

3. Expression of requesting

- Can you ... ? (Ex: can you move here, please?)

- Would you mind ... ? (Ex: Would you mind closing the door, please?

- May i ... ? (Ex: May i sit here?)

- Can i ... ? (Ex: can i borrow your english dictionary?)

4. Expression of possibility

- ... may ... (ex: she may be late)

- Perhaps ... (ex: perhaps she will visit you)

- There's a chance ... (ex: there's a chance we can go home early)

- May be ... (ex: may be she stuck in traffic jam)

5. Expression of giving instruction (similar to commanding which is used to ask someone to do what something that we want)

- Imperative sentence, such as:

Turn off the lamp!

close the door

open the window, please

don't go there

- Instruction of making/doing/operating something

For example: first, boil the water. Then, put two spoons of coffee powder and sugar into the cup. Next, pour the water into the glass after boiling. Then, stir it gently. Finally, a cup of coffee is ready to serve.

Berikut adalah contoh penggunaannya dalam dialog:

1. expression of complaining

Guest: Excuse me.

Receptionist: Yes, sir. What can i do for you?

Guest: I wan to complain about my hotel room's toilet. It's really smelly.

Receptionist: We are so sorry for the inconvenience, sir. I will call the housekeeping staff to your room.

Guest: Alright.

Receptionist: Thank you for your undertanding, sir

2. Expression of proposing

A: Hey, B. what are you doing?

B: Hey. I'm looking for what kind of dance that our dance group should perform on our school anniversary.

A: Oh, why don't we perform a traditional dance.

B: That's a good idea. thanks.

A: No problem.

3. Expression of request

A: Excuse me, sir.

Mr. C: Yes?

A: May i sit beside you?

Mr. C: Sure, the seat is empty

A: Thank you.

Mr. C: You're welcome

4. Expression of possibility

Ann: The movie will start in 15 minutes. Where is Maria?

David: Perhaps she will be late. Her house is quite far from here.

Ann: You're right. but she must be able to manage her time. Let's wait her for 10 minutes.

David: alright.

5. Expression of giving instruction

A: What are you doing, B?

B: I'm looking for a tutorial of turning on the laptop

A: It's easy.

B: Really?

A: Yes. First, you have to press the power button. Then, wait for a minute until the screen show your default account. next, click sign in. Finally, the screen will show your desktop.

B: Thank you. I'll try it.
