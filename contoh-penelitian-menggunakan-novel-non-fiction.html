Compose a Narrative Text Jawaban Pendahuluan

Narrative text adalah suatu tulisan yang kronologis atau merupakan rangkaian peristiwa. Biasanya merupakan suatu cerita rekaan atau fiksi. Narrative text tujuannya adalah untuk menghibur pembaca cerita tersebut.

Pembahasan

Contoh Narrative Text :

Once upon a time there was a snake named Sasha and her fox friend named Wilbur. Wilbur said to Sasha, "Do you want to have lunch with me?" Sasha nodded. They went to Wilbur's house and had lunch together. Wilbur gave Sasha some meat, She tried squeezing it. Wilbur laughed when he saw Sasha, she didn't have any feet. Wilbur said, "Oh Sasha, don't you have any feet? Sasha said madly, "Oh, Wilbur not every animal have to have feet. Then Sasha went home sadly.

The next day She met Wilbur near the old oak tree, their friend,  Oscar came. Sasha told him to call every animal in the forest. He nodded and shouted, "Sasha and Wilbur are having a race!" In only a minute every animal came to watch.

Sasha and Wilbur climbed up, Sasha climbed around the tree while Wilbur tried climbing. When Sasha was at the top she slide down while Wilbur was still on the ground. Wilbur was very sorry for saying Sasha did not have any feet.

Kesimpulan

Moral dari cerita narative diatas adalah every body is unique.

1. These tennis courts aren't used often.

3. A school excursion was being planned.

The teachers were planning a school excursion.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat-kalimat di atas adalah diubah menjadi kalimat aktif atau sebaliknya.

1. Kalimat ini menggunakan passive simple present tense (aren't used).

Active simple present tense: subject + verb 1.

2. Kalimat ini menggunakan active past perfect tense (had + verb 3).

Passive past perfect tense: object + had + been + verb 3 + by subject.

3. Kalimat ini menggunakan passive past continuous tense (was being + verb 3).

Active past continuous tense: subject + was/were + verb -ing.

Jawaban:

30 books altogether

Penjelasan:

# Let the number of non-fiction books initially = a

******************

So, the number of fiction books initially = 3/5 a.

3/5 a + a = 240

3/5 a + 5/5 a = 240

8/5 a = 240

a = 240 : 8/5

a = 240 × 5/8

a = 150

* The number of non-fictions initially initially ==> a ==> 150 non-fiction books

* The number of fiction books initially ==> 240 - 150 = 90 fiction books

**************************************************************************

# Let the number of non-fiction books sold = b

So, the number of fiction books sold = b

********************************************************

# At last, the number of fiction books ==> 90 - b

# At last, the number of non-fiction books ==> 150 - b

********************************************************************************

( 90 - b ) / ( 150 - b ) = 5 / 9

( 5 ) ( 150 - b ) = ( 9 ) ( 90 - b )

750 - 5 b = 810 - 9 b

- 5 b + 9 b = 810 - 750

4 b = 60

b = 60 : 4

b = 15

The number of non-fiction books sold ==> b ==> 15

The number of fiction books sold ==> b == > 15

So, the number of books sold altogether

= 15 + 15

= 30
