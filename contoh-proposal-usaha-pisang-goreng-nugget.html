18. cara membuat pisang nugget tulisan bhs inggris


Answer :
How To Make Bananas Nuggets Chocolate Cheese 
materials :
-Medium-size bananas
- 3 tbsp rice flour
-3 tbsp wheat flour
- 1/2 tsp vanilla essence 
-2 tbsp milk creamer
- grated cheese 
- Oil for deep-frying
-A pinch of salt and a pinch of sugar
-chocolate sprinkles

Steps :
-First, mix sugar, rice flour and wheat flour with water. 
-Second, soak bananas in the batter. Heat the oil in a large wok. 
-Third, Drop one banana at a time into the oil. Cook only two bananas at a time as the temperature of the oil will be drop if there are too many bananas. 
- Fourth, when bananas are crisp and golden brown, remove from oil and drain on a paper towel and put aside. 
- Then, pour the milk creamer on top of the fried bananas. 
- Finally, layer it with grated cheese and sprinkled with chocolate frozen. 

artinya :
Bagaimana Cara Membuat  Nugget Pisang Cokelat Keju
bahan2:
-pisang ukuran sedang
-tepung beras 3 sdm
-3 sdm tepung terigu
-1/2 sdt esens vanila
-susu creamer 2 sdm
-keju parut
-Minyak untuk wajan
-sejumput garam dan sejumput gula
-taburan coklat beku

langkah-langkah:
-Pertama, campurkan gula, tepung beras dan tepung terigu dgn air.
-Kedua, rendam pisang dlm adonan. Panaskan minyak dlm wajan besar.
-Ketiga, masukka pisang satu2 ke dalam minyak. Masak hanya dua buah pisang pada 1 waktu krn suhu minyak akan turun jika terlalu banyak pisang.
- Keempat, ketika pisang yg renyah dan emas kecoklatan, angkat dari minyak dan tiriskan pd sarinagn kertas dan sisihkan.
- Kemudian, tuangkan creamer susu di atas pisang goreng.
- Terakhir, lapisi dengan keju parut dan ditaburi cokelat beku.How to make banana nugget.

Ingredients :
 - 6 to 7 ripe bananas, soften
 - 1 cup shredded fresh coconut - 1 1/2 cups multi-purpose flour - Oil, for frying
 
Steps : 

1. Mash the bananas in a large sized mixing bowl.
2. Add the flour and coconut.
3. Mix the ingredients thoroughly.
4. Heat oil in a pan.
5. Deep fry in the banana mixture, one spoonful at a time.
6. Remove from heat when golden brown.
7. Serve!
